The Shrimati Hansa Mehta Library is the central library of Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda, providing students, faculty, and researchers with access to academic resources, digital platforms, and study spaces. Established in 1950, the library boasts a rich collection of books, journals, and research materials, supporting learning, research, and innovation.
